# UNIVERSAL SPEAKER Pro: Global Seminar AI Suite

## 1. Value Proposition for Speakers
**"Don't let language be a barrier to your global influence."**
With Universal Speaker Pro, you can deliver your expertise to any audience in the world, in their native language, with your own voice and expressions preserved.

## 2. Key Selling Points (The "Wow" Factors)
- **Visual AI Identity:** The audience doesn't just hear a translation; they see you speaking their language. Your lip movements are perfectly synced to the translated audio in 4K.
- **Micro-Broadcast Technology:** No need for expensive headgear. The audience scans a QR code and listens through their own smartphones/earbuds.
- **Emotional Voice Cloning:** It's your voice, your tone, and your passion, translated instantly.

## 3. Product Packages for Sale
### A. The Solo Pro (Per Seminar)
- Real-time Screen Subtitles (up to 3 languages)
- QR Audience Access (up to 100 users)
- basic Voice Cloning

### B. The Global Maestro (Annual License)
- Full 4K Visual Dubbing
- Unlimited Audience Connections
- Professional Custom Voice Profile
- Dedicated Support for Large-scale Conferences

## 4. Operational Workflow for the Seller
1. **Onboarding:** Record 30 seconds of the speaker's voice to create their AI profile.
2. **Setup:** Connect the "Universal Speaker Hub" to the seminar's stage camera and audio feed.
3. **Execution:** Launch the Dashboard, show the QR code on the big screen, and let the AI do the rest.
