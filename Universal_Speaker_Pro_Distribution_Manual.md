## 🛠 2. Setup Guide for Speakers (Organizers)

### Step 1: Initialization
1.  Access the **Official Dashboard** (Navy Blue Interface).
2.  Enable your 4K Webcam and Professional Audio input.
3.  Ensure your **Gemini API Hub** is active (Status: `ENGINE READY`).

### Step 2: Global Broadcast
1.  Select the **Target Language** for the main stage screen.
2.  Click `START GLOBAL BROADCAST`.
3.  The system will instantly calibrate the **AI Visual Sync Overlay** on your live video feed.

### Step 3: Stage Projection
1.  Open the `Stage Screen` view on the venue's master LED wall.
2.  The High-Contrast Academic Layout ensures readability from 100 meters.
3.  The **QR Code** will automatically generate for audience onboarding.

## 📱 3. Audience Onboarding Guide

For a seamless listening experience:

4.  **Listen**: Experience real-time translation in the speaker’s own voice, delivered with zero latency via our cloud speaker hub.
